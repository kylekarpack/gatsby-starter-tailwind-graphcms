Nicole joined WSE as an intern in September 2020 and is in the process of completing her Master's degree in Civil Engineering specializing in hydrology and hydrodynamics at the University of Washington. Her background includes previous work as an energy efficiency engineer and project engineer at the Southern California Gas Company in Los Angeles. In her role at WSE, Nicole is looking forward to learning how to utilize hydrologic and hydraulic modeling and analysis techniques to develop innovative solutions for complex water issues in areas such as stream restoration, flood control, and watershed management.

In her free time, Nicole enjoys hiking, traveling, playing basketball, cooking, and exploring the outdoors.
